Google Play Store App

The purpose of this project was to analyze Google Play Store Apps and see the relation between Number Apps vs Reviews/Ratings avaiable on Google Play store. After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the Google Play store App. Factors we looked at included: App Categories, Average Ratings Per App, Average Rating per Category, and Maximum Reviews Per App.
