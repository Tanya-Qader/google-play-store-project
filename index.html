Google Play Store App

The purpose of this project was to analyze Google Play Store Apps. We wanted to see the relation between Number of Reviews and Types of Apps available.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the Google Play store App. Factors we looked at included: App Categories, Average Ratings Per App, Average Rating per Category, and Maximum Reviews Per App.
